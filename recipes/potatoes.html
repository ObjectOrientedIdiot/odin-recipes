Steamed Potatoes

These are steamed potatoes. They grow out of the ground. You can eat them, for the most part.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Water
  • Pot
  • Steam

Instructions

  1. Cut the potatoes
  2. Pour water into pot
  3. Put potatoes in water in pot
  4. Put the pot with the water and the potatoes inside the water on a stove
  5. Wait until the potatoes are steamed
  6. Take out the potatoes

You now have steamed potatoes, do with them as you please.
