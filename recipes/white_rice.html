White Rice


This is white rice, just in case you've never seen any before. They're like noodles, but whiter.. and ricier.

Ingredients

  • Bag of rice
  • A cup, maybe two
  • Water
  • A functional pot with lid
  • Stove (if using pot)
  • Brain cells (optional, necessity hotly debated)
  • Bowl

Instructions

  1. Measure out some rice into the cup
  2. Make sure the rice does not contain dirt or nuclear weapons (optional)
  3. Add around twice the rice's volume as water
  4. Slowly pour in the rice
  5. Boil pot with lid on
  6. Take off the lid once the rice looks legit, slowly turn down heat
  7. Put finished rice in bowl

That's probably a pretty disgusting bowl of rice you've got there. Don't worry though, it probably wont kill you. Nice job!
