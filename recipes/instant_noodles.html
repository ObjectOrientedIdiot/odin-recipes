Instant Noodles



These are instant noodles, by the way. You know, just in case you haven't seen them before. They're edible, I think.

Ingredients

  • Your instant noodle of choice
  • Water
  • Microwave
  • Access to electricity probably
  • Bowl
  • Pot
  • Working human digits (I think)
  • Stove
  • Seasoning (optional)

Instructions

  1. Use your fingers (optional) to put the pot on your stove
  2. Open pack of instant noodles
  3. Put the block of hard noodles in the pot
  4. Add some water to the pot
  5. Turn up the heat a bit so that you can cook the noodles
  6. When the noodles are no longer a solid brick, turn off the heat and take them out
  7. Put the noodles in a bowl and add some seasoning (to the noodles, not the bowl)

Congrats bro, now you have some seriously scuffed instant noodles. Remember that they are edible even if your eyes say otherwise.
